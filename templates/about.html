About South Cart

Our Mission

To make cross-border shopping safe, affordable, and accessible for every South Sudanese customer.

Who We Are

South Cart is a trusted cross-border e-commerce and delivery platform connecting Uganda and South Sudan. We enable customers in Juba and beyond to order products from Uganda safely and conveniently.

  • ✅ Anti-scam guarantee – no more untrusted Facebook/WhatsApp sellers

  • ✅ Order tracking – know where your package is
  • ✅ WhatsApp support – talk to a real person
  • ✅ Verified suppliers in Uganda

Our Vision

To become South Sudan's most trusted e-commerce and delivery platform, and expand across East Africa.
