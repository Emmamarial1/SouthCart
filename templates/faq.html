Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shopping with South Cart

Q

How do I place an order?

Browse our shop, add items to cart, proceed to checkout, fill in your delivery details, and place your order. You will receive an order number and payment instructions via WhatsApp or SMS.

Q

How do I pay?

We accept MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, and bank transfer. After placing your order, you will receive our payment number. Send the exact amount with your order number as reference.

Q

How long does delivery take?

1-2 days after we confirm your payment. You can track your order status online.

Q

Is South Cart safe?

Yes. We are a registered business. We only work with verified suppliers in Uganda and trusted transport partners. We provide order tracking and WhatsApp support.

Q

What if my product arrives damaged?

Contact us immediately via WhatsApp. We will investigate and resolve the issue.

Q

Can I request a product not listed?

Yes! Use our Request Product form. We will try to source it from Uganda.

Q

Do you deliver outside Juba?

Currently we deliver only to Juba. We plan to expand to other states soon.
